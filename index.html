About me...

Hi, My name is Ziqi Wang, a passionate freelancer.
I love travelling, experience the culture of different countries. Because of that I have many opportunities to try the food of various countries. Among the countries in Asia, Europe, America and Africa I have traveled to, I'm still prefer Asian food. I would like to share my experience and teach those people who are interested in Asian food how to cook it at home.
